Overview

Zyllo is designed to streamline the role request process within your organization. Whether you are a newly hired employee needing access to certain resources or an approver ensuring the right permissions are granted, Zyllo makes the process smooth and efficient. Our platform allows requesters to easily submit role requests and track their status, while approvers can quickly review and approve requests, ensuring that the right people have access to the right resources at the right time.
